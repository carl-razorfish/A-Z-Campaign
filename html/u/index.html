U is for the Udders that fill our bottles with lovely organic milk

 

All the milk used in our coffees, teas, porridges and Happy Meal milk bottles is British, semi-skimmed and organic. Plus we use fresh milk for our ice-creams, milkshakes, and McFlurrys.

Each year we use over 56 million litres of milk and every single drop of it comes from farms that meet the high welfare standards we expect from all our suppliers.
