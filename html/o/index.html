O is for One of your five-a-day, available on our Happy Meal menu

 

We believe there's something for everyone at McDonald's and there’s more to choose from on our Happy Meal menu than ever before. Plus you can find one of your five-a-day in our fruit bags, carrot sticks and orange juice.

Did you know a portion of 4 Chicken McNuggets in our Happy Meals contains around 30% less salt and 32% less fat than in 2003? And you can swap the fries for a fruit bag or carrot sticks for no extra cost.
